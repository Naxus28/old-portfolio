About

This image carousel works with my jQuery plugin. If you like the functionality and want to use it, you may get the code on this gist. The plugin scales to any number of carousels you have on your page.

I am a web developer and designer passionate about digital technologies, the internet, and music. As a developer, I seek to write efficient code and seek to follow the principle of DRY. I am constantly refining my skills and learning new languages and frameworks to develop better sites and applications. As a designer, I prime for clean UI to provide users with a pleasant experience on the practical aspect as well as on the aesthetic. I also love music, photography, and art in general. Before becoming a developer I was a classical pianist and teacher for several years. You can see my piano videos on my youtube channel. Seeking to combine my passion for music and web, in 2011 I launched Zap Músico, a platform for musicians in Brazil. To me it is a priviledge to actively participate in the internet and help to make the world a place where people can freely express their ideas.
